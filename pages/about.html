About Us

Action International is a non-governmental organization committed to transforming the lives of vulnerable communities, especially refugees, displaced individuals, and marginalized populations. Founded on the principles of compassion, dignity, and empowerment, we work tirelessly to restore hope where it's needed most. At Action International, we believe that everyone deserves access to basic human rights—shelter, education, healthcare, clean water, and opportunities for self-reliance.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion

    We serve with empathy, love, and a genuine desire to uplift others.

  • Integrity

    We are transparent, honest, and accountable in all we do.

  • Empowerment

    We build skills, confidence, and independence in every individual we support.

  • Respect

    We honor the dignity and diversity of all people.

  • Collaboration

    We partner with communities, governments, and organizations for greater impact.

  • Excellence

    We are committed to quality, innovation, and continuous improvement in our programs.

Our Mission

To restore dignity and transform lives by providing humanitarian aid, education, health services, and livelihood support to refugees and vulnerable communities—empowering them to thrive with hope and resilience.

Our Vision

A world where every displaced and vulnerable person has the opportunity to live with dignity, access essential services, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our main Goals

  • Support long-term recovery through education, health care, and skills development.
  • Provide immediate relief in times of crisis through food, shelter, and basic needs.
  • Empower individuals with livelihood opportunities and entrepreneurship training.
  • Promote inclusion and protection of refugees and marginalized populations.
  • Advocate for sustainable policies that safeguard the rights and well-being of vulnerable communities.

Most Frequently Asked Questions

What does Action International do?
Action International is a non-governmental organization committed to supporting refugees and vulnerable communities through emergency relief, education, healthcare, skills training, and livelihood programs.
Who are the beneficiaries of your programs?
Our programs benefit refugees, internally displaced persons, women, children, and other vulnerable populations who are affected by conflict, poverty, or displacement.
How can I support or volunteer with Action International?
You can support us by volunteering, donating, or partnering with us. Visit our "Get Involved" page or contact us directly to learn more about current opportunities.
Where does Action International operate?
We operate in regions heavily affected by displacement, primarily in refugee settlements and under-served communities across East Africa, with future plans for broader outreach.

Have a Question?

We're here to help! Whether you're curious about our programs, want to volunteer, or are seeking support, feel free to reach out. Our team is ready to listen and provide the answers you need.

Action International believes in open communication and transparency. Don't hesitate — your questions and ideas matter to us.
